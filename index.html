About Excelra

Where data means more.

Excelra is an informatics company that provides data analytics and scientific informatics services to the global life sciences industry — helping pharmaceutical and biotech organizations turn raw, disparate data into decisions that move research forward.

The company blends deep domain expertise in chemistry, biology, and clinical science with hands-on data engineering — curating, structuring, and harmonizing everything from chemical and genomic data to clinical trial and safety records so it is trustworthy and ready for analysis.

Excelra works across the drug development value chain, from early discovery data curation through clinical and regulatory data services, and increasingly applies AI and cloud-based data engineering to help pharma teams work faster without compromising data quality or compliance.
